Twilio Setup Guide

To use SMS notifications, you'll need a Twilio account and phone number:

Step 1: Create Account

Sign up for a Twilio account if you don't have one. New accounts get free credits to get started.

Step 2: Get Credentials

From your Twilio Console, copy your Account SID and Auth Token. These are found on your account dashboard.

Step 3: Get Phone Number

Purchase a phone number from Twilio that supports SMS. This will be your "from" number for all SMS messages.

Step 4: Configure

Create an SMS configuration here with your credentials, test it, and activate it to start sending SMS notifications.
